About me

My name is Jesse Stern. I am really excited about learning web development. I come from a music background and have been playing and studying music for over ten years. I moved to Marin County, California from Oregon about 3 years ago to study Classical Indian Music at the Ali Akbar College of Music in San Rafael.

I love to learn new things and I am loving learning how to code! Sometimes I try to be a vegan, and do healthy things like yoga and running. You might also catch me binging on pretzels and watching Netflix until I turn into a zombie. I think this class is a productive use of my time.
